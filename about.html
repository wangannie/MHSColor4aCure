Frequently Asked Questions

Will the color ruin clothes or running shoes?
The color does mostly wash out after the event. As with anything dirty, the sooner you wash it the better. For FULL color cleaning ideas and tips, check THIS LINK out!
What happens if there is bad weather?
A little rain won’t stop us! If there are major weather emergencies, we'll notify you if we decide to reschedule or cancel the event.
Are there refunds?
Unfortunately, we can't offer refunds. All proceeds benefit the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
Is the run timed?
The Color 4 a Cure 5K is not timed. We suggest self-timing for anyone desiring a finish time.
